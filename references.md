## Templates and Resources

## Recommended Books and Articles

### Essential Reading for Engineering Managers

**Management Fundamentals**
- [The Manager's Path](https://www.amazon.com/Managers-Path-Leaders-Navigating-Growth/dp/1491973897) by Camille Fournier - The definitive guide for tech management career paths
- [An Elegant Puzzle: Systems of Engineering Management](https://www.amazon.com/Elegant-Puzzle-Systems-Engineering-Management/dp/1732265186) by Will Larson - Systems thinking approach to management
- [The Making of a Manager](https://www.amazon.com/Making-Manager-What-Everyone-Looks/dp/0735219567) by Julie Zhuo - From IC to manager

**Leadership and Culture**
- [Radical Candor](https://www.amazon.com/Radical-Candor-Revised-Kick-Ass-Humanity/dp/1250235375) by Kim Scott - Framework for effective feedback
- [The Five Dysfunctions of a Team](https://www.amazon.com/Five-Dysfunctions-Team-Leadership-Fable/dp/0787960756) by Patrick Lencioni - Building cohesive teams
- [Multipliers](https://www.amazon.com/Multipliers-Revised-Updated-Leaders-Everyone/dp/0062663070) by Liz Wiseman - Amplifying team intelligence
- [Turn the Ship Around](https://www.amazon.com/Turn-Ship-Around-Turning-Followers/dp/1591846404) by L. David Marquet - Intent-based leadership

**Team Organization and Scaling**
- [Team Topologies](https://www.amazon.com/Team-Topologies-Organizing-Business-Technology/dp/1942788819) by Matthew Skelton and Manuel Pais - Organizing teams for fast flow
- [Accelerate](https://www.amazon.com/Accelerate-Software-Performing-Technology-Organizations/dp/1942788339) by Nicole Forsgren, Jez Humble, and Gene Kim - Science of high-performing organizations
- [The Phoenix Project](https://www.amazon.com/Phoenix-Project-DevOps-Helping-Business/dp/1942788290) by Gene Kim - DevOps novel (surprisingly good)

**Strategy and Decision Making**
- [Good Strategy Bad Strategy](https://www.amazon.com/Good-Strategy-Bad-Difference-Matters/dp/0307886239) by Richard Rumelt - What makes strategy effective
- [High Output Management](https://www.amazon.com/High-Output-Management-Andrew-Grove/dp/0679762884) by Andrew Grove - Intel CEO's management wisdom
- [The First 90 Days](https://www.amazon.com/First-90-Days-Strategies-Expanded/dp/1422188612) by Michael Watkins - Transitioning into new roles
